Official Resources & References

This guide is based on official EU legislation, regulatory guidance, and authoritative industry analysis. For detailed technical requirements and legal text, consult these primary sources:

Primary EU Legislation & Guidance

  • Regulation (EU) 2023/956 – The core CBAM regulation establishing the carbon border adjustment mechanism (EUR-Lex Official Text)
  • Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2023/1773 – Detailed implementing rules for the transitional phase (EUR-Lex Official Text)
  • European Commission CBAM Portal – Official registry, guidance documents, and FAQs (EC CBAM Homepage)
  • CBAM Transitional Registry – Platform for reporting and authorization (CBAM Registry)
  • Commission Guidance Documents – Technical guidance on methodology, verification, and reporting (Official Guidance Library)

Industry Analysis & Expert Commentary

  • KPMG CBAM Analysis – Comprehensive compliance guides and sector-specific insights (KPMG CBAM Hub)
  • PwC CBAM Resources – Practical implementation strategies and financial impact assessments (PwC CBAM Insights)
  • Deloitte CBAM Tracker – Regulatory updates and compliance timelines (Deloitte CBAM Resources)
  • EY CBAM Compliance Center – Technical methodology guidance and data management tools (EY CBAM Hub)

Stay Updated

CBAM regulations and implementing acts are subject to ongoing refinement. The European Commission regularly publishes updated guidance, FAQs, and technical clarifications. Bookmark the official CBAM portal and subscribe to regulatory updates to ensure you're working with the latest requirements.
