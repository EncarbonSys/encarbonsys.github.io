The Regulatory Landscape

Six Mandatory EU Obligations

Every exporter must meet these six statutory requirements to maintain permanent EU market access.

01

O3CI Portal Registration

Establishing presence on the official operator portal to maintain verified registry data.

02

Monitoring Plan

Drafting a CBAM-compliant Monitoring Methodology Document—the bedrock of your compliance.

03

Embedded Emissions

Tracking direct and indirect emissions with audit-defensible records across all processes.

04

Annual CBAM Reports

Producing and submitting reports in official EU formats every reporting period without fail.

05

Third-Party Verification

Coordinating reviews by EU-accredited independent verifiers to ensure regulatory trust.

06

SEFA & Carbon Price

Calculating allocations and carbon pricing logic to avoid double taxation penalties.
