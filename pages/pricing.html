The Compliance Landscape

Six Mandatory EU Obligations

01

O3CI Registry Entry

Registration on the official EU operator portal to manage installation-specific carbon data.

02

Monitoring Plan (MP)

Development of a CBAM-compliant methodology document in English, detailing all production processes.

03

Emissions Monitoring

Recursive tracking of direct and indirect embedded emissions across the entire production chain.

04

Unified CBAM Reports

Generation of quarterly and annual reports in EU-mandated XML and PDF formats.

05

Independent Audits

Coordination with EU-accredited verifiers for mandatory site visits and data verification.

06

SEFA Calculations

Precise calculation of free allocations and carbon prices paid in home countries to optimize costs.

Is This For You?

Self-Service is Ideal For:

  • Teams with dedicated finance or sustainability staff.
  • Companies wanting full hands-on control of their data.
  • Exporters with established data collection processes.

Managed Service is Ideal For:

  • Exporters lacking in-house CBAM calculation expertise.
  • Businesses exporting to multiple EU buyers consistently.
  • Teams wanting to outsource reporting with oversight.

CaaS Elite is Ideal For:

  • Enterprises seeking 100% regulatory certainty.
  • Exporters wanting to delegate the entire operational burden.
  • Businesses scaling EU exports rapidly across borders.
