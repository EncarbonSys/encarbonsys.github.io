The Global Landscape

Six Mandatory EU Obligations

01

O3CI Portal Entry

Registration on the EU's official operator portal to sync installation data with the CBAM registry.

02

Technical Monitoring Plan

Preparation of a mandatory Monitoring Methodology Document defining all system and process boundaries.

03

Continuous Monitoring

Recursive calculation of actual specific embedded emissions (SEE) for every production cycle.

04

Annual CBAM Reporting

Generation and submission of statutory periodic reports using official EU XML/PDF templates.

05

Audit Coordination

Facilitating mandatory site visits and data reviews by an EU-accredited independent verifier.

06

Cost Optimization (SEFA)

Calculation of free allocations and carbon price offsets to minimize unnecessary border tax exposure.
