The Problem

The EU's Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) is non-negotiable. If you're exporting steel, aluminium, fertilizers, cement, hydrogen, or electricity — you must submit emission reports. No valid XML = No entry to the EU.

Shipment Delays
Missing CBAM reports can delay your shipments and disrupt supply chains
Penalties from Customs
Non-compliance results in hefty fines and customs penalties
Lost Buyers
EU buyers won't risk non-compliant suppliers

Most MSME's lack the tools. EnCBAM enables CBAM compliance - fast, credible and export-ready.
