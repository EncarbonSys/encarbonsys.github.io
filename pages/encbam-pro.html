The Problem

CBAM Reality

CBAM is not just a reporting problem. It is a data, process, and accountability problem.

What stakeholders are struggling with today:

  • Fragmented data across Excel, ERP, emails, invoices, meters, and PDFs
  • No monthly emissions discipline → panic at quarter/year-end
  • Zero audit trail → high rejection and verification risk
  • Confusion around precursors, attribution, and system boundaries
  • Manual CBAM templates that break when scale increases
  • Consultants spending time compiling data instead of advising

"CBAM today feels like tax compliance in 1998. EnCBAM modernizes it."
